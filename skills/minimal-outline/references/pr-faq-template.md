## How to Use This Document

Write the Press Release as if the product launches today. The FAQ addresses questions from customers (External) and internal stakeholders (Internal). Circulate drafts, expect bar-raising questions, and iterate until the narrative is compelling. Only then move to planning and implementation.

It's not unusual to write ten drafts or more and meet with senior leaders five times or more to iterate and refine.
