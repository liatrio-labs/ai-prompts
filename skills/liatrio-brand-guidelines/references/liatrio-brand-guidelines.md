## Typography

Our type system uses DM Sans throughout to maintain consistency across all communications.

### DM Sans

**Category:** Primary Typeface

A geometric sans serif font that balances readability with modern aesthetics. Our primary typeface for all brand communications.

**Foundry:** Google Fonts

**Source:** Google Fonts

**Usage Guidelines:** Use for all text including headlines, body copy, and UI elements. Provides excellent readability at all sizes with a full range of weights from Thin to Black.

**Available Weights:** Thin, ExtraLight, Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, ExtraBold, Black

[View Font →](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Sans)

### Type Scale Targets

- H1: 48-60px
- H2: 36-40px
- H3: 28-32px
- Body: 16-18px

## Brand Usage Guidelines

Essential principles for maintaining brand consistency.

### Logo Usage

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the `L` in Liatrio. Never distort, rotate, or alter logo colors. Use the appropriate logo variant for the background.

### Color Usage

Primary Green (`#24AE1D`) is the hero color and should be used prominently in key brand moments. Grey 800 (`#111111`) provides strong contrast for text. Use Bright Green (`#89df00`) sparingly for accents and highlights. For dark mode, use `#1a1f23` as the primary background and `#1E2327` for cards and elevated surfaces.

### Typography Hierarchy

DM Sans is the primary typeface for all text. Use bold and semibold weights for headlines and hero text. Use regular and medium weights for body copy and UI elements. Maintain consistent sizing: H1 (48-60px), H2 (36-40px), H3 (28-32px), Body (16-18px).

### Accessibility

Ensure sufficient color contrast (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text). Never use color alone to convey information. Provide alternative text for all images and logos.
