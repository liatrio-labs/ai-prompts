## Brand Guidelines

Essential principles for maintaining brand consistency

### Logo Usage

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the 'L' in Liatrio. Never distort, rotate, or alter the logo colors. Use the appropriate logo variant for your background.

### Color Usage

Primary Green (#24AE1D) is our hero color and should be used prominently in key brand moments. Grey 800 (#111111) provides strong contrast for text. Use Bright Green (#89df00) sparingly for accents and highlights. For dark mode, use #1a1f23 as the primary background and #1E2327 for cards and elevated surfaces.

### Typography Hierarchy

DM Sans is our primary typeface for all text. Use bold and semibold weights for headlines and hero text. Use regular and medium weights for body copy and UI elements. Maintain consistent sizing: H1 (48-60px), H2 (36-40px), H3 (28-32px), Body (16-18px).

### Accessibility

Ensure sufficient color contrast (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text). Never use color alone to convey information. Provide alternative text for all images and logos.
